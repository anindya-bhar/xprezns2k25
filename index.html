Chakratan : The Wheels of Change

Bosco XpreZnS is the annual school fest of Don Bosco School Bandel. First held in the year 2008, Bosco XpreZnS has been held in a successful manner each year since then barring the year 2020 because of the ongoing Covid 19 pandemic. However, quite notably, Bosco XpreZnS was one of the only inter school fests held online in the following year, with 10 schools taking part in 14 events taking place online on different video meetings. XpreZnS urges you to express yourself, to discover your inner potential and elevate yourself on the grandest stage - the world itself.

With the same message, here we are, for the 17th time, in the grandest way possible. Welcome to change. Become the change.

Welcome, to Bosco XpreZnS 2k24.
